# Learning Log - DevX Companion

Local learning log for DevX Companion specific lessons.

Learnings that apply across multiple projects or represent fundamental insights should be marked for promotion to outer-world-context.

---

## Promotion Criteria

Mark a learning for promotion by adding `cross_pollinate: true` or including keywords like "fundamental", "core", "essential", "pattern", or "principle" in the learning entry.
